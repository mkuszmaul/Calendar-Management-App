Instructions on running the app in GUI mode:
By default, the GUI mode will create and select a calendar called "Default Calendar"
in your timezone.

Navigating and viewing the selected calendar:

- On the left side of the window, observe the calendar view of the app. This shows
the current month. Navigate to different months using the arrows in the top left.

![img.png](imagesForTutorial/img.png)

- On the right side of the window, observe the schedule view of the app. This shows
the next ten (or fewer) events in text form starting from the selected date. Change the date
by selecting and editing the textbox, or modify it one year at a time with the small arrows
to the right of the textbox. To refresh the schedule view (for example, after creating a new
event), you can modify or refresh the textbox.

![img_1.png](imagesForTutorial/img_1.png)


Creating events:
- To create an event, click on the day you would like the event to start on in the calendar
view. A popup will appear asking you enter event details.
- Enter your event details in the popup. Subject, start time, and end time are mandatory, but
location and description are optional. If you attempt to create an event without an appropriate
subject or start and end time, you will get another popup preventing you.

![img_2.png](imagesForTutorial/img_2.png)

- Here is an example of a possible event you might create. When you are done, press "create",
or "cancel" if you changed your mind.

![img_3.png](imagesForTutorial/img_3.png)

- Now refresh the schedule view and observe your new event!

![img_4.png](imagesForTutorial/img_4.png)

Creating and switching calendars:
- By default, the program creates and selects one calendar called "Default Calendar"
  in your timezone. To add more calendars, press the "+" button to the right of the calendar
dropdown menu. You should get the following popup:

![img_5.png](imagesForTutorial/img_5.png)

- Enter your desired calendar name and select a timezone from the dropdown menu. Press "create"
to make and select this calendar or "cancel" if you changed your mind.

- To change which calendar is selected, go to the dropdown menu and click your desired calendar.
![img_6.png](imagesForTutorial/img_6.png)
